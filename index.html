🖥️ Windows 11 is Here – Is Your Clinic Ready?

Microsoft is ending support for Windows 10 in October 2025.
That means now is the time to start planning your upgrade to Windows 11 — before it becomes urgent.

✅ How to Check if Your Device is Compatible

  1. Download the PC Health Check Tool
    Get it directly from Microsoft: https://aka.ms/GetPCHealthCheckApp
  2. Run the tool
    It will tell you if your device meets the system requirements for Windows 11.

⚕️ Why Doctors Should Consider Upgrading

  • Faster & More Reliable – Optimized performance on newer hardware.
  • Modern Security – Better protection for sensitive patient data.
  • Improved Multitasking – Snap layouts and virtual desktops for easier charting and EMRs.
  • Long-Term Compatibility – Stay ahead as medical software evolves.
  • Peace of Mind – Avoid last-minute stress and ensure smooth transitions.
